About

A decade at 35,000 feet.
Now grounded in what matters.

I am Hazik Fayaz — a Line Check Cabin Crew Evaluator at SpiceJet, a certified NLP Practitioner, and a life coach based in Bengaluru. My decade in aviation has taught me one thing above all else: how people behave under pressure is not a personality trait. It's a learnable, changeable skill.

"What aviation taught me about people, NLP gave me the tools to change."

After years of evaluating crew performance, conducting proficiency checks, and coaching cabin crew through high-stakes scenarios, I began to see the same patterns repeating — not just in the air, but in every domain of life. Limiting beliefs that looked like confidence problems. Communication failures that were really rapport failures. Decisions made from fear dressed up as logic.

Through NLP, coaching, and the storytelling voice I've been building across LinkedIn, YouTube, and Twitter, I now help professionals untangle those patterns — and fly higher.
